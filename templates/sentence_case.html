Frequently Asked Questions

What is sentence case formatting?

Sentence case formatting capitalizes the first letter of each sentence while keeping the rest of the text in lowercase. It's the standard style used in most formal and online writing for readability and clarity.

How do I convert text to sentence case online?

To convert text to sentence case, paste your content into the input box above and click "Convert Text." The tool will instantly format your text so that each sentence starts with a capital letter.

Can this tool handle paragraphs and multiple sentences?

Yes! Our Sentence Case Converter works for single sentences, full paragraphs, or entire articles. It detects sentence boundaries and applies proper capitalization automatically.

Will this tool remove existing capital letters in names or titles?

No — the tool converts everything to lowercase except for the first letter of each sentence. You may need to manually adjust proper nouns, acronyms, or specific brand names afterward for accuracy.
