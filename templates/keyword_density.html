Frequently Asked Questions

What is keyword density in SEO?

Keyword density refers to the percentage of times a keyword or phrase appears in a block of text compared to the total word count. Maintaining a balanced keyword density helps improve SEO without triggering search engine penalties for keyword stuffing.

How do I check keyword density in my content?

To check keyword density, paste your content into the tool above, enter the keyword you want to analyze, and click "Calculate Density." The tool will instantly display the keyword density as a percentage.

What is a good keyword density for SEO?

Most SEO experts recommend a keyword density of around 1% to 2% for optimal results. This ensures your target keywords are present enough to signal relevance without overloading your content and risking penalties.

Can this tool check multiple keywords at once?

Currently, this tool analyzes one keyword at a time to provide the most accurate density percentage for each individual term. You can repeat the process with different keywords as needed for your content optimization.

Is my text or keyword information stored when using this tool?

No, your text and keyword data are processed locally in your browser session and are not stored or transmitted, ensuring complete privacy and security.
